Welcome


GAF Traffic is a people-centred traffic engineering consultancy serving new and existing projects in Western Australia. 

welcome

GAF Traffic is a people-centred traffic engineering consultancy serving new and existing projects in Western Australia. We provide comprehensive solutions to all traffic engineering issues that emerge in new projects as well as existing developments.

With a focus on a positive outcome for all road users, we use our expertise to help people safely get to where they need to go. 

From consultation and planning, through to detailed assessment, we provide a range of traffic engineering services that support intelligent and functional transportation infrastructure in Western Australia.

We work to maintain strong relationships with government agencies and private industry around the country, guided by our desire to provide excellent customer service and effective solutions that will benefit the wider community.

GAF Traffic is led by Andrew McDougall. To have a chat with Andrew about how your project or event can run as smoothly and safely as possible, get in touch today.

Who are GAF Traffic?

Our business started in early 2013, working from a home office. It was an environment that reflected our purpose as a proudly family-run business, providing for the needs of:

•         local government,

•         state government agencies,

•         small business, and

•         private developers.

 

We exist to provide a quality service to our clients in addition to placing priority on family and volunteer efforts.

Our team has been involved in projects throughout WA for the last 10 years. We’ve also been involved in smaller projects across the rest of Australia. We carry experience at the ground-level of transport – with a history covering postal delivery through to truck driving – so we thoroughly understand the practical challenges facing our clients.
